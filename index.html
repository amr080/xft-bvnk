Frequently asked questions.

How is BVNK licensed?

BVNK is regulated as an EMI in the UK and Europe and holds multiple VASP registrations in Europe. Please note that cryptoassets are not regulated in the UK by the FCA. We're committed to expanding our licensing footprint globally. Learn more about our licenses.

How much does BVNK’s platform cost?

We charge fees to merchants for using our services and our pricing tiers are based on payment volumes. Our aim is to operate with fair and transparent margins and to help you succeed financially. Please get in touch with our team to receive a bespoke pricing proposal.

What can I do with BVNK’s platform?

BVNK provides stablecoin payments infrastructure, unifying banks and blockchains in a single API platform, to accelerate global money movement. We enable businesses to send, receive, convert, and store stablecoins and fiat across a variety of domestic and international payment rails and blockchains. 

Who is BVNK for?

BVNK provides stablecoin payments infrastructure, unifying banks and blockchains in a single platform, to accelerate global money movement. We cater for businesses looking to process at least €500,000 in payments a month, with a minimum of 6 months of trading/operating history.

Is BVNK safe?

At BVNK, we hold multiple licenses and regulatory approvals, including EMI licenses in the UK and Europe, and VASP licenses in Europe (please note that cryptoassets are not regulated in the UK by the FCA). Around 1/4 of our team is dedicated to legal, risk and compliance and the function is represented at C-level. Our team has deep expertise in digital assets and payment system regulatory compliance frameworks. Our robust compliance controls mitigate risk, while our multilayered AML systems fight financial crime. Learn more about our approach to compliance.
