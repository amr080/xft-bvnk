Mastering the crypto payments experience in Gaming

9 ways to boost conversion and player loyalty

‍

We process billions in crypto payments each year – and this is what we’ve learnt. This guide is designed to help you boost payment conversion rates, avoid payment failures and improve the experience for your players when they deposit with crypto.

Download your copy to get best practices and actionable advice from the BVNK product team on:

  • Optimising for mobile
  • Localisation
  • Important details specific to crypto payments to prevent failures and lost funds
  • Tips on showing currency conversions
  • How wallet integrations can make the journey smoother
  • Instructional/UX prompts
  • Handling under and over payments from your customers
  • Enabling VIP player deposits any time.
