Frequently asked questions.

Is it easy to integrate with BVNK?

Integrating the BVNK payments API can take a week or less. Our dedicated solutions engineers will work with you throughout the process. You can use utilise pre-built components and elements or build natively with a full API integration.

What can I do with BVNK’s platform?

BVNK provides stablecoin payments infrastructure, unifying banks and blockchains in a single API platform, to accelerate global money movement. We enable businesses to send, receive, convert, and store stablecoins and fiat across a variety of domestic and international payment rails and blockchains.
