Frequently asked questions.

How is BVNK licensed?

BVNK is regulated as an EMI in the UK and Europe and holds multiple VASP registrations in Europe. Please note that cryptoassets are not regulated in the UK by the FCA. We're committed to expanding our licensing footprint globally. Learn more about our licenses.

Is it easy to integrate with BVNK?

Integrating the BVNK payments API can take a week or less. Our dedicated solutions engineers will work with you throughout the process. You can use utilise pre-built components and elements or build natively with a full API integration.
