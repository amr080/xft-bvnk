Frequently asked questions.

How does BVNK store digital assets?

While your digital assets are in our custody, we provide protection through robust systems, processes and technology. We take a multi-layered approach, including separate record-keeping (we never mix different types of funds), storing exactly what you deposit without converting it, and using advanced security measures like multi-step verification, data encryption and ongoing risk monitoring. Learn more about BVNK security features.

‍

Who is BVNK for?

BVNK provides stablecoin payments infrastructure, unifying banks and blockchains in a single platform, to accelerate global money movement. We cater for businesses looking to process at least €500,000 in payments a month, with a minimum of 6 months of trading/operating history.

How does BVNK safeguard customer e-money funds?

We segregate (hold separately) customer e-money funds at all times from BVNK’s company funds or the company funds of our banking partners. This means that your e-money funds are protected in the unlikely event that BVNK or one of our e-money partners, becomes insolvent.

BVNK operates as an Electronic Money Institution in the UK via System Pay Services Limited (company number 11621763, authorised and regulated by the Financial Conduct Authority – Ref: 901057). We operate as an EMI in Europe via System Pay Services Malta (company number C66961 (authorised and regulated by the Malta Financial Services Authority).

How does BVNK help me meet my AML and KYC obligations? 

Our multi-layered approach to AML and KYC helps you mitigate financial crime risk. We deploy a combination of best-in-class tools and our proprietary machine learning models for effective crime detection and prevention. Our Financial Intelligence team is experienced in leading investigations, and our case management portal makes it easy for you to track and manage held payments.

How will I know if a payment is flagged or held, and how can I respond?

You can track and manage your held payments and respond to Requests For Information (RFIs) from BVNK in our easy-to-use case management portal. There’s no need for lengthy email exchanges and we guarantee a response within 24 hours, though our average response time is 10 minutes.

How safe are crypto payments? How can you mitigate financial crime risk?

Specialist blockchain analytic tools are often used as part of an effective anti-financial crime framework, alongside a number of other measures, to help reduce financial crime risks. Learn more in this video from BVNK and Chainalysis.

Is BVNK ISO 27001 certified?

Yes, BVNK is ISO 27001:2022 certified. This is an enterprise-grade stamp of approval on BVNK’s security controls, and demonstrates our commitment to maintaining the highest possible security standards for our customers. We’re certified by The British Assessment Bureau, a UKAS-accredited certification body, backed by the UK Government. As part of the ISO 20071 accreditation, BVNK is committed to being independently audited every year to ensure we continue to meet these standards. You can learn more about BVNK’s security controls in our Trust Centre. Verify our accreditation at any time.

How is BVNK licensed?

BVNK is regulated as an EMI in the UK and Europe and holds multiple VASP registrations in Europe. Please note that cryptoassets are not regulated in the UK by the FCA. We're committed to expanding our licensing footprint globally. Learn more about our licenses.
