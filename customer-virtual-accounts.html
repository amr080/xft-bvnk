Frequently asked questions.

What are customer virtual accounts?

By virtual accounts, we mean individual vIBANs in the name of your customers, attached to a payment account issued by us to you. This allows you to send and receive payments on behalf of your customers in their name.

Which businesses can access issuance of customer virtual accounts?

This product is available for businesses that are based in the UK and in countries supported by BVNK outside the EEA region, and the US. GBP account availability is limited to businesses incorporated in the UK, Isle of Man, Jersey, Gibraltar, or Guernsey.

Is BVNK safe?

At BVNK, we hold multiple licenses and regulatory approvals, including EMI licenses in the UK and Europe, and VASP licenses in Europe (please note that cryptoassets are not regulated in the UK by the FCA). Around 1/4 of our team is dedicated to legal, risk and compliance and the function is represented at C-level. Our team has deep expertise in digital assets and payment system regulatory compliance frameworks. Our robust compliance controls mitigate risk, while our multilayered AML systems fight financial crime. Learn more about our approach to compliance.

Is it easy to integrate with BVNK?

Integrating the BVNK payments API can take a week or less. Our dedicated solutions engineers will work with you throughout the process. You can use utilise pre-built components and elements or build natively with a full API integration.

How is BVNK licensed?

BVNK is regulated as an EMI in the UK and Europe and holds multiple VASP registrations in Europe. Please note that cryptoassets are not regulated in the UK by the FCA. We're committed to expanding our licensing footprint globally. Learn more about our licenses.

How much do customer virtual accounts cost?

Please get in touch with our team to receive a bespoke pricing proposal, and to understand how we structure our pricing. Our aim is to operate with fair and transparent margins and to help you succeed financially. Transfers between your customer’s virtual accounts and your wallets powered by BVNK are at no additional cost.
